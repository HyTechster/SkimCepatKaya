    How to play

    • Tap to earn money. Every tap adds to your balance (the big number) and your net worth (your total, shown up top).
    • Net worth never drops, so spending does not hurt your leaderboard rank. It ranks by net worth.
    • Shop, Side Hustle: makes each tap worth more money.
    • Shop, Auto Investment: earns money on its own over time, even while the game is closed.
    • Shop, Boosters: a temporary multiplier on your taps.
    • Shop, Methods: upgrade from Piggy Bank to Wallet, QR DuitSekarang, Debit Card, Bank, and Vault. Each has a higher multiplier. Switch to the one you own.
    • Shop, Ranks: prestige titles from Rakyat up to Bilionair. Each unlocks at a level (higher net worth) and, once bought, permanently adds a flat amount to your money per tap. Your rank shows up top and on the leaderboard.
    • Grab the cash: click a floating cash drop for a bonus. The reward grows as you level up Side Hustle.
    • Idle income: your Auto Investment keeps earning while you are away. Come back to collect it.

    Everything saves to your account automatically.
